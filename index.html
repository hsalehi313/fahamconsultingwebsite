About Us

Welcome to FAHAM Business and IT consulting firm. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services to your personal, business and IT needs, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.

Founded in 2015 by Amil Ali with FAHAM Accounting Services Inc. and has come a long way from its beginnings in Toronto, Ontario. Currently, our team of 3 includes a couple of Chartered Professional Accountants and an IT specialist, and our prime focus is to help small or medium sized business owners by guiding and providing them with IT and business consulting services so they can flourish in their particular business segment. In addition, we encourage and help new immigrants to Canada who are interested to invest, open their own business or work from home, and avoid all the hassles and struggles a normal immigrant has to go through.

When we first started out, we found the services we provide are readily available, however most of them are not to the point and business consultants in the market are trying to see their personal benefits rather than client’s interest, which drove us to help those people out who are desperate to improve their business needs.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Amil Ali, CPA, CGA - CEO, FAHAM Business Consulting Services

Services

FAHAM Consulting works with business owners with 10 to 100 employees to help them grow their business, move with confidence and make quicker decisions. We help our clients strengthen their business by providing basic four pillars but core services Business consulting, Accounting, IT, Marketing - providing them with a firm foundation on which to grow.

By working alongside business owners to help them focus on their purpose, priorities become clear. The right services create leverage for the business owner; they have room to breathe again. Consistent process allows an owner to focus only on the outliers, and let their team handle the routine. Results become more predictable. With those basic services in place, business owners know the engine is running smoothly, and they should spend their time on focusing running the business.
